Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2025

VetteQuiz ("we", "us", or "our") operates corvettequiz.com. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. We believe in keeping things simple and honest.

Information We Collect

When you use VetteQuiz, we may collect the following:

  • Account information — if you create an account, we store your username and email address
  • Google profile — if you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google
  • Quiz scores — your daily scores, XP earned, and streaks are stored to power the leaderboard and stats
  • Usage data — basic information like your play dates and performance statistics

Guest players who do not create an account have their scores stored anonymously under a randomly generated guest name. No personal information is collected from guests.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your username and rank on the leaderboard
  • To track your daily streak and XP progress
  • To show you your personal stats (games played, average score)
  • To send account-related emails (email confirmation, password reset)

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes. We do not use your data for any purpose other than operating the quiz.

Third-Party Services

VetteQuiz uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider. Your account data and scores are stored on Supabase servers. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Google OAuth — if you choose to sign in with Google. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — used to host this website. See GitHub's Privacy Policy.

Cookies and Local Storage

VetteQuiz uses your browser's local storage to remember your streak, whether you've played today, and your session. This is essential for the quiz to function. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies of any kind.

Data Retention

Your account data and scores are retained as long as your account exists. Guest scores may be cleaned up periodically. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

Children's Privacy

VetteQuiz is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of VetteQuiz after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
